About Expense Splitter

Making shared expenses simple and fair

What We Do

Expense Splitter helps you manage shared costs with friends, roommates, and groups. Whether it's a trip, household bills, or dinner with friends, we make it easy to track who paid what and who owes whom.

Key Features

Group Management

Create groups for different occasions - roommates, trips, events, or any shared expense scenario.

Smart Splitting

Split expenses equally or customize shares. Our system automatically tracks balances for you.

Balance Tracking

See at a glance who owes money and who is owed. No more confusion or forgotten debts.

Settlement System

Record payments between members and watch balances update automatically.

Built With

  • Flask - Backend Framework
  • Bootstrap 5 - UI Components
  • SQLite - Database
  • jQuery - Client-side Validation
  • HTML5 & CSS3 - Modern Web Standards
  • Jinja2 - Templating Engine
