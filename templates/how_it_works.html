How It Works

Follow these simple steps to manage shared expenses

1

Create a Group

Start by creating a group for your shared expenses. Give it a name (e.g., "Apartment Roommates" or "Goa Trip") and add all the members who will be splitting costs.

Tip: You need at least 2 members to create a group.
2

Add Expenses

Whenever someone pays for something shared, add the expense to the group. Enter the description, amount, and who paid for it.

Tip: You can split equally or customize shares for each member.
3

Track Balances

Our system automatically calculates who owes whom. Check the group's balance section to see who's owed money and who needs to pay.

Tip: Balances update in real-time as you add expenses.
4

Settle Up

When someone pays back what they owe, record the settlement. The balances will automatically adjust to reflect the payment.

Tip: All settlements are recorded in the history for transparency.

Example Scenario

Weekend Trip with Friends

  1. Create a group called "Goa Weekend" with Alice, Bob, and Charlie
  2. Alice pays ₹6,000 for the hotel → Add expense: "Hotel booking" (split equally)
  3. Bob pays ₹1,500 for dinner → Add expense: "Dinner at Beach Shack" (split equally)
  4. Check balances: Alice is owed ₹4,000, Bob is owed ₹500, Charlie owes ₹4,500
  5. Charlie pays Alice ₹4,000 → Record settlement
  6. New balances update automatically!

Quick Split Calculator

Don't need to save the group? Use the Quick Split calculator on the home page for one-off calculations. Just enter the total amount and number of people, and we'll tell you how much each person owes.
